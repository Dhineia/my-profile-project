About Me

I am a dedicated and passionate professional who graduated in Mobile Application & Web Development during Senior High School, achieving High Honors. I then went on to earn a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology (BSIT). During my final year in college, my team was awarded the Best in Capstone Project, and I consistently made the Dean's List from the 1st year to the 4th year.

My professional journey began during my 4th year of college when I joined Crossfuze/Trec Global as a Customer Service Representative in 2022. Through hard work and dedication, I was recognized as the Top Performer of the Year in 2023 by both my company and our clients, leading to my promotion to Senior Agent.

In addition to my full-time job, I have a thriving freelancing career where I create visually appealing and user-friendly websites, as well as insightful data analysis reports. I am a lifelong learner, always seeking to enhance my knowledge and capabilities.
